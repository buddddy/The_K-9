Frequently Asked Questions

What is The K-9 Support Centre?

We are a non-profit organisation that helps aid canines in need across the globe.

Where is The K-9 Support Centre?

We're based in Manchester, UK. You can find our address and how to contact us in our Contact page.

What services do you provide?

We are a non-profit organization that carries out fundraisers and sell products to help animal charities and charities. 100% of our proceeds go towards helping dogs in need.

Can our charity become a partner of The K-9?

Absolutely! We are proud to help as many dog-loving charities as possible. Please contact us via email or phone if you wish to work with us.

How can I help?

Please check out our Partners and consider donating to them if you support their mission. Also, feel free to explore our products if you wish to contribute to us.
